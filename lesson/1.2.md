# 1.2: mjri ia pesimjrike sapi

in lagirulo, we add '**ha**' or '**lo**' after a **noun** to express its degree, then it could be a **adjective** or another **noun**.

| lagirulo | english |
| :------: | :-----: |
|   gjni   | gender  |
|  gjnilo  | female  |
|  gjniha  |  male   |

> moxiha, mo ia mjri.
> 
> *happy, i am mary.*

> moxiha djtabeni, si ia wan.
> 
> *happy afternoon, he/she is wax.*

> mjri ia pesimjrike sapi.
> 
> *mary is america people.*

> wax ia pesizhon sapi.
> 
> *wang is chinese.*

## yes / no

when the question is asking for yes / no. ha = yes, lo = no.

> ma mjri ia gjnilo?
> 
> ha, mjri ia gjnilo.
> 
> *if mary is girl?*
> 
> *yes, mary is girl.*

ma zhax ia pesinihox sapi?
lo, si ia pesizhox sapi.
> if zhang is japan people?
> no, he/she is china people.
