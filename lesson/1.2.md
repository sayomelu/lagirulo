# 1.2: mali ya marika sapi

in lagirulo, we add '**ha**' or '**lo**' after a **noun** to express its degree, then it could be a **adjective** or another **noun**.

| lagirulo | english |
| :------: | :-----: |
|   gjni   | gender  |
|  gjnilo  | female  |
|  gjniha  |  male   |

moxiha, mo ya mjri.
> happy, i am mary.

moxiha djtabeni, si ya wan.
> happy afternoon, he/she is wan.

mjri ya pesimjrike sapi.
> mary is america people.

wan ya pesizho sapi.
> wang is chinese.

ma zhan ya pesiniho sapi?
> if zhang is japan people?

karelo, si ya pesizho sapi.
> not correct, he/she is china people.
